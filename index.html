Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Astronomy from New Mexico State University, who worked with Professor Anatoly Klypin. My dissertation was to explore the techniques used to estimate the mass of galaxy clusters using weak gravitational lensing, using simulations from the MultiDark suite.

If you are interested in weak lensing or simulation surface density codes, check out my codes page or my Github page.

I am currently moving out of the field of astronomy, and moving into the field of data science. In the works is a project page where I will showcase my skills at analysis and predictive modeling using machine learning. I've also created a Kaggle profile, and am excited to start taking on their competitions.
