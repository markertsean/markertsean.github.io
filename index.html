Welcome!

I am Ph.D. recipient in Astronomy from New Mexico State University, working with Professor Anatoly Klypin. My thesis work was to study the systematic effects of weak lensing density profile fitting of galaxy clusters, using simulations from the MultiDark suite.

If you are interested in weak lensing or simulation surface density codes, check out my codes page or my Github page.

I've also created a Kaggle profile, and am excited to start taking on their competitions. If you're looking for a teammate, I'm looking to explore and learn!
